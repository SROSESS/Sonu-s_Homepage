Week 2

Rant About Technology

Urssula K.le Guin

I can't imagine a world living without technology for me. As a 2000s kid growing up in the rise of technology, from my computer to having a laptop that I am typing on now, I can't imagine a world without it. But sometimes I have heard from my grandparents saying, "Can you live without your technology for once?" That kind of made me realize how life without technology could have been. Without a smartphone or any electrical appliance, how would people live with it? I guess it might have been easier during that time since they had to rely on other things even though it was hard. But I think living in today's world, technology plays a huge role in everyone's life.
